Sentiment Analysis through text

Discovering Music with Emotion: Understanding Your Mood: Ever wondered how your favorite music apps know just the right song to play when you're feeling a certain way? It's all about understanding your mood! Our system uses advanced technology to analyze two key aspects of your emotions: the text you input and even your facial expressions. Text Analysis: When you tell us how you're feeling, we don't just take your word for it. We use something called sentiment analysis to understand the emotion behind your words. It's like having a friend who knows exactly how you're feeling, even when you don't say a word!
