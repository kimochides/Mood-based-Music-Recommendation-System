MUSIFY:
A mood based music recommendation app

A mood-based music recommendation system is a technology designed to suggest songs or playlists to users based on their current emotional state or preferences. This system aims to enhance user experience by providing personalized music selections that resonate with their current emotional state, helping to set the desired atmosphere or evoke particular feelings.
