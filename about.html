Understanding Your Mood

Ever wondered how your favorite music apps know just the right song to play when you're feeling a certain way? It's all about understanding your mood! Our system uses advanced technology to analyze two key aspects of your emotions: the text you input and even your facial expressions.

How it works?

Simply share your mood and favorite genre, and our algorithms get to work, analyzing your input and facial expressions. We then scour Spotify's vast library to find tracks that match your mood and preferences. Finally, we deliver personalized recommendations, ensuring you discover the perfect songs, whether it's upbeat pop or soothing jazz.

What's Next?

We're always working to make your music discovery experience even better. Have ideas or feedback? We'd love to hear from you! Get in touch with our support team anytime for assistance or to share your thoughts on how we can improve.
